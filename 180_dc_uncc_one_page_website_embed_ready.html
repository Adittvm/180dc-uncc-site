About Us

180 Degrees Consulting at UNC Charlotte (180DC UNCC) is a chapter of the world’s largest student‑run consultancy. We partner with nonprofits and social enterprises to solve strategic, financial, and operational challenges. Members gain real‑world experience while developing leadership and teamwork skills aligned with careers in consulting, finance, analytics, and beyond.
